About us
Olá! We’re Carlos and Helena, a couple with over 50 wonderful years together. Our journey led us to this beautiful seaside town, where we’ve created a home filled with laughter, countless adventures, and cherished memories.

Just a stone’s throw from the golden sands and within easy reach of Lisbon, the capital, our home has been the backdrop for family gatherings, sports, and sunsets. We’re thrilled to invite you to be part of our story.

Whether you’re here for a beach holiday, surfing adventure, or to unwind, we’ll make your stay feel like home. Costa da Caparica offers surfing, kiteboarding, cycling, beach volleyball, and more—all just steps away. And with Lisbon nearby, you can easily explore the vibrant city life before returning to the tranquility of the coast.

Our doors are open to new friends. Come in, kick off your shoes, and let Costa da Caparica’s beauty sweep you off your feet.

With warm regards,
Helena & Carlos
